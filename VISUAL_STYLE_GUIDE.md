# Visual style guide

Matter Siege is a nocturnal miniature alchemy workshop photographed at tabletop scale.

- Warm fire and brass highlights contrast with cool moonlit stone.
- Geometry is chunky, beveled, readable, and physically plausible.
- Materials have distinct roughness, reflectivity, edge response, and silhouettes.
- Effects are energetic at impact and dissipate quickly enough to preserve aim readability.
- UI resembles precision laboratory instrumentation: dark glass, fine rules, ivory type, and elemental color.
- Avoid neon sci-fi panels, flat mobile-game gradients, and large opaque HUD blocks over the battlefield.
