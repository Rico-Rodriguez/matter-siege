# Content rules

- Material IDs and recipe component IDs are stable protocol values.
- Every material requires a strength, weakness, readable color, impact VFX, and break VFX.
- No material may dominate density, HP, toughness, and resistance at once.
- Status effects must be expressible through the shared runtime fields.
- JSON is canonical; renderer materials may interpret visual hints but may not change rules.
- Competitive content must remain unlock-neutral. Progression may change selection breadth, not hidden stats.
